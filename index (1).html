How Plateful Purpose works

  • Notify

    Hotels and restaurants with excess food alert us.

  • Collect

    Our team gathers surplus food from participating establishments.

  • Inspect

    We ensure all donations meet safety and quality standards.

  • Distribute

    Food is delivered to shelters, food banks, or directly to individuals in need.

  • Our Story

    Plateful Purpose is a nonprofit organization dedicated to fighting hunger by redistributing surplus food to those in need within our community. Founded in 2020, we recognized the urgent need to address food insecurity while also minimizing food waste. Our mission is simple yet profound: to ensure that everyone has access to nutritious food and to create a sustainable solution for our community.
